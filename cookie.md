## Ingredients
- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Mix butter and sugar until smooth.
3. Add vanilla and dry ingredients.
4. Fold in chocolate chips.
5. Bake for 10–12 minutes.
